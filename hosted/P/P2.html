Turning pages. The ever-fading scent of paper. A world of words.

As a child, you would often get lost in endless pages and worlds, spending hours and afternoons lost in a book. As an adult, you've forsaken the physical page for the digital versions, easy to carry, a veritable library in your pocket. Despite this, a part of you misses the scent of an old, crinkly book and the comfort of curling up on your couch. You just don't have enough time these days.

There's never enough known.
